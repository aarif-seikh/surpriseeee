A story brighter than the stars, pretty like the moon, and forever to be there like the Sun. Stars? Who are the stars in my life? Your eyes — your eyes are the stars... who guide me through college, my eyes look up to these stars to survive. Moon? Where is this moon? Is this moon in the corridor or is it in my class?
